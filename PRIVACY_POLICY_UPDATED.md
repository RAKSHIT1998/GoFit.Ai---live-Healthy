# GoFit.Ai - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 20, 2026

## 1. Introduction

GoFit.Ai ("Application", "Service", "We", "Us", or "Our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

When you register and use GoFit.Ai, we collect the following personal information:

#### Profile Information:
- Name
- Email address
- Date of birth / Age
- Profile picture (optional)
- Fitness goals
- Activity level

#### Physical Metrics:
- Current weight
- Height
- Target weight
- Age and gender

#### Health & Fitness Data:
- Dietary preferences (vegan, vegetarian, keto, etc.)
- Food allergies and restrictions
- Favorite cuisines and foods
- Cooking skill level
- Budget preference for meals
- Workout preferences and types
- Available workout time
- Motivation level
- Lifestyle factors (drinking frequency, smoking status)
- Fasting preferences

#### Activity Data:
- Meal logs and history
- Nutritional data for logged meals
- Workout history and performance
- Water intake logs
- Apple HealthKit data (with your permission)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:
- Device information (device model, OS version)
- App usage analytics
- Crash reports and error logs
- Authentication tokens and sessions

### 2.3 Third-Party Data

We integrate with:
- **Apple Health** - With your explicit permission to read health data
- **Apple Sign In** - For authentication
- **In-app Purchases** - Via StoreKit for subscriptions

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Service Delivery

- Generate personalized meal and workout recommendations
- Track your progress toward fitness goals
- Sync data with Apple Health
- Process subscriptions and in-app purchases
- Provide customer support

### 3.2 AI-Powered Recommendations

**IMPORTANT:** To provide personalized meal and workout recommendations, we share the following data with **OpenAI (GPT-4o API)**:

#### Data Shared with OpenAI:
- **User Profile**: name, age, goals, activity level
- **Physical Metrics**: weight (kg), height (cm), target weight, target calories, target macro nutrients
- **Dietary Data**: dietary preferences, allergies, food restrictions, favorite cuisines, favorite foods
- **Onboarding Preferences**: cooking skill, budget preference, workout preferences, meal timing preference
- **Machine Learning Insights**: learned user type, discovered favorite foods, preferred meal times, average meal calories, preferred macro ratio
- **Recent Activity**: Last 5 meals logged with items, calories, and macronutrients

OpenAI uses this data **only** to generate personalized recommendations and does not permanently store your personal information. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy at https://openai.com/privacy-policy for details on their data handling practices.

**Your Control Over AI Sharing:**
- You are informed about AI data sharing through our in-app privacy disclosure
- You can view detailed information about what data is shared before accepting recommendations
- You can opt-out of AI-powered recommendations at any time in Settings
- Disabling AI recommendations prevents data sharing with OpenAI

### 3.3 Analytics and Improvement

- Understand how users interact with the app
- Improve app features and user experience
- Debug technical issues
- Conduct research on health and fitness trends

### 3.4 Communication

- Send you notifications about recommendations and reminders
- Respond to your inquiries
- Send important updates about the Service
- Marketing communications (with your consent)

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Third-Party Services

We share personal data with the following third-party services:

#### OpenAI (GPT-4o API)
- **Purpose**: Generate personalized meal and workout recommendations
- **Data Shared**: User profile, fitness goals, dietary preferences, recent meal history
- **Privacy**: https://openai.com/privacy-policy
- **Opt-Out**: You can disable AI recommendations in Settings

#### Apple Services

#### Analytics Services
- **Firebase Analytics**: App usage analytics (optional, can be disabled)
- **Sentry**: Error reporting and crash analytics

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect the safety of our users
- Protect our rights and property

### 4.3 Business Transfers

If GoFit.Ai is involved in a merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice of any such change.

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal data:

- **Encryption**: All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
- **Authentication**: Secure authentication with JWT tokens
- **Database Security**: Encrypted password hashing (bcrypt)
- **Access Control**: Role-based access to sensitive data
- **Regular Security Audits**: We periodically review our security practices

### 5.2 Limitations

While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we maintain industry-standard protections.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format
- Contact us at [support@gofitai.org] to exercise these rights

### 6.2 Correction and Deletion

- You can update your profile information directly in the app
- You can request deletion of your account and associated data
- After deletion, some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes

### 6.3 Opt-Outs

- **Marketing Communications**: Unsubscribe from promotional emails
- **Push Notifications**: Disable in device settings or app settings
- **Analytics**: Disable analytics tracking in Settings

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

Contact us at [privacy@gofitai.org] to exercise these rights.

### 6.5 GDPR Rights (EU/UK Residents)

If you're in the EU or UK, you have rights under GDPR:
- Right of access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object

Contact us at [privacy@gofitai.org] to exercise these rights.

## 7. Data Retention

### 7.1 Retention Periods

- **Account Data**: Retained while you have an active account
- **Meal/Workout History**: Retained for 2 years or as long as you use the app
- **AI Recommendation Requests**: Not permanently stored by OpenAI
- **Analytics Data**: Retained for 12 months
- **Backup Data**: Retained for 30 days after deletion

### 7.2 Data Deletion

When you delete your account:
- Your profile and personal information are deleted
- Your meal and workout history are deleted
- Your subscriptions are canceled
- Some aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for analytics

## 8. Children's Privacy

GoFit.Ai is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information promptly.

## 9. International Transfers

These countries may have data protection laws that differ from your home country. By using GoFit.Ai, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may provide a different level of data protection.
